Submit an issue/ticket/bug report

IRC/Text chat

You can almost always find developers and other users on live chat on IRC. Please note timezones and work hours, so if nobody is active when you ask your question, leave your client logged in, and someone will respond when they can. And once you get your question answered, feel free to stay a while and enjoy the banter while you can help and listen in on other questions and Singularity related banter.

  • Server: irc.freenode.net
  • Channel: #singularity
